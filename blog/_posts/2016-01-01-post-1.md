Happy new year, happy new site! I have been curious about [Jekyll](http://jekyllrb.com/), 
a static blog generator, for a long time, so I finally decided to try it. It was a fun experience, 
getting to know a little bit about Ruby, Liquid, Sass, ..., plus some javascript and CSS.

The initial setup was mostly straight forward. But it took me a while to get the 
[Bootstrap](http://getbootstrap.com/) navigation bar and footer to look good. The pagination also 
did not look good initially. Ok, good enough now. The source files that generated this site is available 
on [GitHub](https://github.com/jiafulow/jiafulow.github.io).
