I am currently a postdoctoral associate at the Department of Physics at the [University of Florida][1]. 
I am a member of the <i class="fa icon-cms fa-lg"></i> [Compact Muon Solenoid][2] (CMS) collaboration, 
which is a particle physics experiment at the world's highest energy at the 
<i class="fa icon-cern fa-lg"></i> [CERN][3] [Large Hadron Collider][4]. 
I am working on the CMS Phase 2 endcap muon trigger upgrade project. 

I received my Bachelor's degree in Physics from the [University of Nebraska-Lincoln][5], 
and my Ph.D. degree in Experimental Particle Physics from the [University of Florida][6]. 
The subject of my dissertation is the search for the standard model Higgs boson 
decaying to bottom quarks in the associated production mode with a Z boson 
decaying to neutrinos ([link][7]).
